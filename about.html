About Namuna Law Associates

Namuna Law Associates Private Limited is a full-service law firm based in Pokhara, Nepal. We are a team of dedicated advocates and legal professionals committed to providing high-quality legal services with integrity and professionalism.

Our firm was established with a vision to offer comprehensive legal solutions across a wide spectrum of practice areas, combining traditional legal expertise with modern research and technology.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional legal services with integrity, professionalism, and a deep understanding of our clients' needs, while contributing to the development of the legal profession in Nepal.

Our Objectives

As per our Memorandum of Association, we are committed to:

  • Legal Activities – Providing legal services, advocacy, and legal consultation
  • Research & Development – Social science and humanities research
  • Administrative Support – Office administration and support services
  • Documentation – Photocopying, document preparation, and office support
  • Publishing – Legal publications and research papers
  • Professional Services – Professional, scientific, and technical activities
◆

Our firm is guided by the vision of Prof. Dr. Gyaneshwer Parajuli and Dr. Shobha Devi Poudel, who bring together decades of legal experience, academic excellence, and a deep commitment to justice.

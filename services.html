Our Services

Corporate & Commercial Law

We provide expert guidance on company formation, partnership deeds, shareholder agreements, merger and acquisition advisory, corporate compliance, and regulatory filings under the Companies Act, 2006. Our team ensures that your business operations are conducted with legal certainty and in full compliance with applicable laws.

Civil & Criminal Litigation

Our advocates represent clients in civil disputes including contracts, property, torts, and debt recovery, as well as criminal proceedings, bail applications, and defense throughout the justice process. We are committed to providing strategic and effective representation at all levels of the Nepali court system.

Property & Land Law

We assist with land title due diligence, property registration, dispute resolution, and land acquisition advisory. Our expertise ensures that property transactions are conducted with legal certainty and security, protecting your interests in all real estate matters.

Family & Inheritance Law

We handle matrimonial matters, divorce proceedings, child custody disputes, succession planning, wills, and estate administration with sensitivity and professionalism. Our approach is compassionate yet effective, ensuring that your family's interests are protected.

Constitutional Law

Our team possesses deep expertise in constitutional matters, fundamental rights protection, and public interest litigation. We are committed to upholding the principles of justice and equality enshrined in the Constitution of Nepal.

Legal Research & Advisory

We provide in-depth legal research, social science analysis, and expert opinions for individuals, businesses, and government agencies. Our research supports informed decision-making and policy development, ensuring that you have access to the highest quality legal intelligence.

Documentation & Office Support

We offer comprehensive documentation services including drafting of legal documents, contracts, affidavits, power of attorney, photocopying, document preparation, and notarization. Our meticulous approach ensures that all your legal documents are prepared with precision and care.

Publication & Knowledge Sharing

We produce legal publications, research papers, and professional training resources for the legal community. Our commitment to knowledge sharing contributes to the development of the legal profession and promotes greater understanding of the law.
